Character Creation

Roll ability scores.

  1. Roll 3d6 for each ability, and record them on you character sheet:
    • Strength (STR)
    • Intelligence (INT)
    • Wisdom (WIS)
    • Dexterity (DEX)
    • Constitution (CON)
    • Charisma (CHA)
  2. Calculate your bonus for each ability, and record them on your character sheet:
    • 3: -3
    • 4-5: -2
    • 6-8: -1
    • 9-12: 0
    • 13-15: +1
    • 16-17: +2
    • 18: +3
  3. Record the effects of the bonus for each ability:
    • STR: Strength
      • Melee attack rolls...
      • Melee damage rolls...
        • Note: A penalty here will never reduce the damage for a successful attack below 1.
    • INT: Intelligence
      • Number of languages known...
        • Note: A penalty here means the character cannot read more than a word or two, and knows only their native language.
      • Modifies saving throws against "illusion" effects.
    • WIS: Wisdom
      • Some saving throws vs. magical attacks, particularly those affecting the character's will...
    • DEX: Dexterity
      • Missile attack rolls...
      • Armor Class...
      • Initiative...
    • CON: Constitution
      • Hit die rolls...
        • Note: A penalty here will never reduce the result of any hit die below 1.
      • Modifies saving throws vs. poison.
    • CHA: Charisma
      • Reaction rolls...
      • Number of retainers (4 + CHA bonus)...
      • Morale of retainers (7 + CHA)...

Select a race.

  • Dwarves
    • Ability Restrictions:
      • CON: Minimum 9
      • CHA: Maximum 17
      • Large weapons: May not employ weapons more than four feet in length (specifically two-handed swords, polearms, and longbows).
    • Bonuses:
      • Darkvision, 60 ft....
      • Detect slanting passages, traps, shifting walls and new construction on a roll of 1-2 on 1d6 (search roll)...
      • +4 saving throw vs. Death Ray or Poison, Magic Wands, Paralysis or Petrify, and Spells
      • +3 saving throw vs. Dragon Breath
    • Class Restrictions:
      • May be Clerics, Fighters, or Thieves.
  • Elves
    • Ability Restrictions:
      • INT: Minimum 9
      • CON: Maximum 17
      • Never roll hit die larger than d6.
    • Bonuses:
      • Darkvision, 60 ft....
      • Detect secret doors on a 1-2 on 1d6 rather than the usual 1 on 1d6 (search roll)...
        • Detect secret doors 1 on 1d6 (cursory look)...
      • Immune to the paralyzing attack of ghouls...
      • Reduce the chance of surprise by 1 in 1d6...
      • +2 saving throw vs. Magic Wands and Spells...
      • +1 saving throw vs. Paralysis or Petrify...
    • Class Restrictions:
      • May Clerics, Fighters, Magic-Users, or Thieves; may also combine Fighter and Magic-User (combination class) if they meet the requirements for both classes.
  • Halflings
    • Ability Restrictions:
      • DEX: Minimum 9
      • STR: Maximum 17
      • May not wield large weapons.
      • Must wield medium weapons with both hands.
      • Never roll hit die larger than d6.
    • Bonuses:
      • +1 attack rolls with ranged weapons
      • +2 to AC when attacked in melee by creatures larger than "man-sized..."
      • +1 initiative
      • 90% chance to hide outdoors...
      • 70% chance to hide indoors...
      • +4 saving throw vs. Death Ray or Poison, Magic Wands, Paralysis or Petrify, and Spells
      • +3 saving throw vs. Dragon Breath
    • Class Restrictions:
      • May be Clerics, Fighters, and Thieves.
  • Humans
    • Ability Restrictions:
      • None.
    • Bonuses:
      • +10% Experience
    • Class Restrictions:
      • May be any single class.

Select a class.

  • Cleric
    • Ability Restrictions
      • WIS: Minimum 9
      • Blunt Weapons: May only use blunt weapons (specifically including warhammer, mace, maul, club, quarterstaff, and sling).
    • Skills
      • Turn Undead:
    • Spells
      • No spells at first level.
    • Hit Points
      • 1d6 + (CON Bonus)
  • Fighter
    • Ability Restrictions
      • STR: Minimum 9
    • Hit Points
      • Dwarf, Human: 1d8 + (CON Bonus)
      • Elf, Halfling: 1d6 + (CON Bonus)
  • Magic-User
    • Ability Restrictions
      • INT: Minimum 9
      • Weapons: May only use a dagger and/or a walking staff (or cudgel.)
      • Armor: May not wear armor of any sort nor use a shield, as they interfere with spellcasting.
    • Spells
      • Read Magic
      • One other 1st–level spell
    • Hit Points
      • 1d4 + (CON Bonus)
  • Thief
    • Ability Restrictions
      • DEX: Minimum 9
      • Armor: May not wear metal armor as it interferes with stealthy activities.
    • Skills
      • Open Locks: 25%
      • Remove Traps: 20%
      • Pick Pockets: 30%
      • Move Silently: 25%
      • Climb Walls: 80%
      • Hide: 10%
      • Listen: 30%
    • Hit Points
      • 1d4 + (CON Bonus)

Roll starting money.

  • 3d6 x 10 gp

Purchase equipment.

  • Refer to "Cost of Weapons and Equipment" and "Explanation of Equipment" to decide what equipment your character will buy. Some items to consider:
    • Weapons
    • Armor
    • Provisions

Pre-calculate armor class.

  • AC: Armor Class of the armor you purchased plus any relevant racial bonuses plus DEX bonus.

Pre-calculate attack/damage rolls.

  • Melee Attack: Attack Bonus + STR Bonus
  • Ranged Attack: Attack Bonus + DEX Bonus
  • Melee Damage: Attack Bonus + DEX Bonus

Calculate saving throws.

  • Refer to the table of "Saving Throws" by class.
  • Apply any racial bonuses to those saving throws.
