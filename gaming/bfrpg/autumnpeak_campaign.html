Autumnpeak Campaign

Preface

Notes about how a GM might use this book.

Autumn

Autumnpeak

Give background information for Autumnpeak.

King of Autumn's Harvest

The full adventure.

Autumn Adventure Seeds

Level 1-2 adventure seeds that flesh out Autumnpeak's background, history, and the lifestyle of the residents. Establish a relationship with the town guard to solve various minor crimes that each give a small slice-of-life to the adventurers?

Winter

Trading and Smuggling

Give background for trading and smuggling in Autumnpeak.

Season of Storms

The full adventure.

Winter Adventure Seeds

Level 2-3 adventure seeds that flesh out commerce, business, and smuggling in Autumnpeak. Perhaps breaking "protection" rackets? Corporate espionage?

Spring

Autumnpeak Bay

Give background for Autumnpeak harbor, the isle the keep is on, and the role the keep plays in the defense of the harbor.

Faringray Keep

The full adventure.

Spring Adventure Seeds

Level 3-4 adventure seeds that flesh out the harbor, perhaps dealing with pirates? Perhaps sea monsters? Perhaps some missions leveraging the keep/castle building rules in the core rule book to repair the keep they've won?

Summer

Unrest Abroad

Give background information for the home continent, including political unrest.

Under Siege

The full adventure.

Summer Adventure Seeds

Level 4-5 adventure seeds that firm up Autumnpeak's political situation coming out of the siege. Is Autumnpeak independent now from overseas rule? Or do missions need to be run with Autumnpeak to reaffirm Autumnpeak's loyalty to the overseas crown?
